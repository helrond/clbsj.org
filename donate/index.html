The "Re-Potting" Fund

As we collectively make our way through these unprecedented times, it has been said that "every organization is now a startup." The CLBSJ Board is embracing this reality, and has recently decided to “re-ground” with a focus on two priorities: (1) solidifying our participation in local and regional organizing in the Lower Hudson Valley, where our flagship collection is located, and (2) forging online encounters between Biblically-based scholars and activists that speak into the needs of this time.

In order to pull off this plan, we need your help! CLBSJ is entirely funded by donations from individuals, and we need to raise $10,000 to make our budget for the rest of 2020. A generous donor has offered to match all gifts made to the "re-potting fund," up to $5,000. Please help us reach this goal!
