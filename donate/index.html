Other ways to give:

  • Donate stock: CLBSJ is able to receive donations of stock. Please reach out to us to coordinate the details.
  • Organize a gift through your employer: some employers have programs to facilitate matching gifts or other forms of support for organizations their staff are involved with.
  • Encourage your church to give: If you are a pastor or church leader, you could encourage your church to designate a gift from your mission fund, continuing education fund, or another fund.
  • Donate books: CLBSJ is only able to accept carefully curated book donations due to space limitations.
  • Donate your time: CLBSJ is always looking for volunteers!

To discuss any of these options, or to suggest other ideas, please email info@clbsj.org

CLBSJ is recognized by the IRS as a 501(c)(3) organization, and contributions are tax deductible to the extent allowable by law.
