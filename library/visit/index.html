We have a world-class collection of books, periodicals and other materials relating to biblical study and social justice.

The collection is available for use by scholars and activists from all walks of life, regardless of institutional or religious affiliation. Because we want our materials to be available at any time for research, we do not lend out books. The collection can be consulted on site during regular opening hours.

Hours

The library is open 9am to 5pm, Tuesday to Saturday.

Please contact us if you are unable to visit the library during these hours and we may be able to make special arrangements for you.
